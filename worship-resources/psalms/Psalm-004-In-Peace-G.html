Psalm 4

In Peace I Will Lie Down
South Florida Psalter | Key: G | BPM: 60

Verse 1

G D/F# Em Answer me when I call to you, C G my righteous God. D/F# Em Give me relief from my distress; C D have mercy on me and hear my prayer.

Verse 2

G D/F# Em How long will you people turn my glory C G into shame? D/F# Em How long will you love delusions C D and seek false gods?

Chorus

C G Know that the Lord has set apart D/F# Em his faithful servant for himself; C G the Lord hears when I D/F# Em call to him.

Verse 3

G D/F# Em Tremble and do not sin; C G when you are on your beds, D/F# Em search your hearts and be silent. C D Offer the sacrifices of the righteous G and trust in the Lord.

Verse 4

G D/F# Em Many, Lord, are asking, "Who will bring us prosperity?" C G Let the light of your face shine on us. D/F# Em Fill my heart with joy when C D their grain and new wine abound.

Chorus

(Repeat Chorus)

Bridge

C G In peace I will lie down and sleep, D/F# Em for you alone, Lord, C G D/F# Em make me dwell in safety. C D G (2x)

Outro

| G | (let ring)
