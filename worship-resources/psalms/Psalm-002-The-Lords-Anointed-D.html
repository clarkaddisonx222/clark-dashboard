Psalm 2

The Lord's Anointed
South Florida Psalter | Key: D | BPM: 76

Verse 1

D A/C# Bm Why do the nations conspire G D and the peoples plot in vain? A/C# Bm The kings of the earth rise up G A and the rulers band together D A/C# against the Lord and against Bm G A his anointed, saying,

Verse 2

D A/C# Bm "Let us break their chains G D and throw off their shackles." A/C# Bm The One enthroned in heaven laughs; G A the Lord scoffs at them. D A/C# He rebukes them in his anger Bm G A and terrifies them in his wrath, saying,

Chorus

G D "I have installed my king A/C# Bm on Zion, my holy mountain." G D I will proclaim the Lord's decree: A/C# Bm He said to me, G D A/C# Bm "You are my son; G A D today I have become your father.

Verse 3

D A/C# Bm Ask me, and I will make the nations G D your inheritance, A/C# Bm the ends of the earth G A your possession. D A/C# You will break them with a Bm G rod of iron; D A/C# you will dash them to pieces Bm G A like pottery."

Verse 4

D A/C# Bm Therefore, you kings, be wise; G D be warned, you rulers of the earth. A/C# Bm Serve the Lord with fear G A and celebrate his rule with trembling.

Bridge

Bm G Kiss his son, or he will be angry D A/C# and your way will lead to your destruction, Bm G for his wrath can flare up in a moment. D A/C# Bm G Blessed are all who take A D refuge in him.

Chorus

(Repeat Chorus 2x)
